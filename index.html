Shadow of the Titanic

In the early hours of 15 April 1912, the Titanic sank, leaving 1,500 people struggling in the freezing Atlantic. The screams faded to silence as hypothermia claimed lives. "Surviving the Titanic" explores the impact on the 705 survivors, shedding light on their lives post-tragedy. This book, timed for the 100th anniversary, reveals how the disaster shaped the survivors' futures and memories.
